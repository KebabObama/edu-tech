Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to the venue?

The EduTech Day 2025 will be held at the Innovation Center Prague. You can reach it by public transport or car. See the Venue & Tickets section for a map.

Are tickets refundable?

Tickets are non-refundable, but you may transfer your registration to another attendee. Contact us at info@edutechday.com.

Will the sessions be recorded?

Yes, recordings of the sessions will be available to all registered participants after the event.
